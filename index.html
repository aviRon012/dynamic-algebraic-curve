Dynamic Algebraic Curve

This is a visualization of Implicit Algebraic Curves, defined by the polynomial equation f(x,y) = 0.

Unlike standard graphers, the curve is not pre-defined. Instead, a swarm of autonomous particles moves organically, and in every frame, a linear algebra solver calculates the unique curve that passes through all of them.

Tip: Pause the simulation to drag the particles and reshape the curve manually!
